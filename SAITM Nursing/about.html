About SAIN

St. Andrews Institute of Nursing is located in Delhi (NCR). It is spread across 20 acres of lush green campus with the globally acknowledged infrastructure. The Classrooms are centrally air-conditioned equipped with modern technology for teaching. Good Education encompasses instilling Moral, Aesthetic, Athletic and Intellectual values in the citizens of tomorrow. St. Andrews is the best place to achieve this kind of education. Affiliated to ABC , this college has lush green surroundings which provides a conducive environment for the students to grow not only as good medicals but as world class citizens too.The Teaching and Non-Teaching Staff of the Institute is a blend of Senior Experienced and Young, Dynamic faculty members devoted to the noble cause of education. We, at St. Andrews look towards creating a workforce that lives and breathes the same spirit. We give importance to Practical Training, Self-Learning, Coupled with Discipline, which attracts students from overall India.
