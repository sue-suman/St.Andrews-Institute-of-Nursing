B.Sc (Hons)

BSc Hons or Bachelor of Science Honours is a 3-year undergraduate program that covers a wide range of disciplines in science such as Biology, Agriculture, Computer Science, Geography, Chemistry, etc. Anyone from the science stream can pursue this course with a minimum aggregate of 45-50% in Class 12 from a recognized board.

Eligibility A minimum of 50% in Class 12 with Science stream
Admission Process Merit Based or Entrance Examination

B.Sc (Post Basic)

BSc Nursing (Post Basic) is a 2 years undergraduate course that is designed to enhance the nursing skills of professionals who already have a background in nursing or a similar field. It provides an advanced level of knowledge related to medical care. The basic eligibility criteria are that students should have completed Class 12 with a certificate in General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) and should have a registration in Registered Nurses and Registered Midwives (RNRM).

Eligibility A certificate in General Nursing and Midwifery and registered as R.N.R.M (Registered Nurse Registered Midwife) with the State Nurses Registration Council.
Course Fees INR 20,000-1,00,000
Average Salary INR 3-5 LPA
Job Positions Registered Nurse, Nursing Instructor, Staff Nurse, Rehabilitation Specialist, Critical Care Nurse, Nurse Manager, Community Health Nurse (CHN) and many more.
Top Recruiting Companies AIIMSCare, Hospitals, Apollo Hospitals, Fortis Healthcare, Medanta, Max Healthcare, Manipal Hospitals, Aster DM Healthcare, Narayana Superspeciality Hospital
Scope Diverse Career Opportunities, High Demand, Continuing Education Opportunities.

M.Sc

MSc Nursing is a 2 year postgraduate course in nursing, clinical speciality and clinical practise. MSc Nursing syllabus is designed to skill and educate students advanced knowledge in the field medical care and treatment. Students learn about nursing research & statistics, nursing management, pediatric nursing, cardiological nursing, medical-surgical nursing etc. Students who want to take admission in MSc Nursing should have completed their bachelor's or undergraduate degree in BSc Nursing or Post-basic BSc Nursing with a minimum score of 55%. The ones with a post-basic degree should also have work experience of one year before or after post-basic BSc nursing.

Eligibility Bsc nursing or its equivalent degree
Course Fees INR 80,000
Average Salary INR 3 Lakhs per annum
Job Positions Registered nurse, nursing in charge, nursing supervisor, nursing executive, nurse educator, a paramedic nurse, certified professional coder, clinical nurse manager, nursing director, etc.
Top Recruiting Companies Fortis, Max Healthcare, Columbia Asia, Apollo, Dr. Lal Path Labs
Scope The course offers jobs as a nurse educator and can also indulge in the research work.
